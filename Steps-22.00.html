Begin by creating a Region as New Group. To do this, right click on State and choose 'New Group'. The Region will have the Midwest, Northeast, Southeast, Southwest, and West. The respective States for each Region are as follows:
Midwest-> Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Kansas, Michigan, Minesota, Missouri, Nebraska, Ohio, South Dakota and Wisconsin.
Northeast-> Connecticut, Delaware, Maine, Maryland, Massachusetts, New Hampshire, New Jersey, New York, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island and Vermont.
Southeast-> Alabama, Arkansas, District of Columbia, Florida, Georgia, Kentucky, Louisiana, Mississippi, North Carolina, South Carolina, Tennesse, Virginia and West Virginia.
Southwest-> Arizona, New Mexico, Oklahoma and Texas.
West-> Alaska, California, Colorado, Idaho, Montana, Nevada, Oregon, Utah, Washington and Wyoming.
