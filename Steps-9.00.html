Importing Data 9

Dataset link: Gun Violence Incidents in the United States | Kaggle

1.    After importing your data, go ahead and save it on your device.
2.    Open Power BI Desktop.
3.    Click on Get data ribbon
4.    Select Excel Workbook and then click connect
5.    Choose Mass Shootings and click open
6.    Select the table, a preview of data will be displayed, click on Load.
