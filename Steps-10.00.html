Transforming Data in Power Query Editor 10
In this step you will clean and transform the data:
1.  Select Transform Data from Home tab
2.  Click on the down arrow of Column 6 & 7 and select the “Rename” then click OK, to change the column names to "Fatalities" and "Injuries" respectively.
4.  Click on File and select Close and Apply. Then select the Incident Date column and click the Format tab in the home ribbon and select the mm/dd/yyyy.
